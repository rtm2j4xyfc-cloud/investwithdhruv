The Story

Like a lot of Canadians, I didn't grow up with a strong foundation in personal finance. Nobody explained TFSAs to me in school. Nobody walked me through what an ETF actually is. The information existed ‚Äî it just wasn't organized, accessible, or built for the Canadian context.

Every time I searched for guidance, I found resources written for Americans ‚Äî different accounts, different tax rules, different platforms. The Canadian experience was almost invisible online.

So I did the work myself. I spent years reading, researching, and testing ideas until I had a clear picture of what actually works for a regular Canadian investor. And then I thought ‚Äî why should everyone have to figure this out alone?

Invest With Dhruv is the resource I wish existed when I started ‚Äî Canada-specific, clearly written, and designed so you can walk into any financial decision with genuine confidence.
